The Murder of Shane Nguyen

Content Warning

The content of this timeline goes into indepth detail about the murder of Shane Nguyen, which involved dismembering of his body. Some readers may find information provided in the “more information” disturbing.

April 23, 2021

Shane Nguyen is reported missing and a statewide Silver Alert is Issued.

April 25, 2021

5:10AM

Shane Nguyen’s Vehicle is seen by Fort Wayne Police Department Sergeant C. Young and is momentarily pursued in a high speed chase.

After a while Shane Nguyen’s vehicle is found crashed in the backyard of a home and a black trash bag is found in the back of the van.

Nguyen’s body is found in the black trash bag and receipts to Lowes and Walmart are found in the van that leads detectives to what may have happened to Nguyen.

Three Males are identified in connection to Nguyen’s Murder.

A Social Media Post leads detectives to a storage unit that Nguyen may have been murdered at.

Content found in the storage unit indicates that Nguyen was murdered there.

April 26, 2021

All Three Suspects are in custody.

Cody Clements had turned himself into authorities for questioning.

Jacob Carreon-Hamilton states that he has no involvement and had no prior knowledge of what happened.

Matthew Cramer confesses his (and Carreon-Hamilton’s) involvement with Nguyen’s death.

Cramer and Carreon-Hamilton are formally charged.
