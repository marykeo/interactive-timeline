<p>On April 23rd Shane Nguyen was reported missing by his family after he had not returned home. A statewide Silver Alert was issued by Fort Wayne Police Department in need of help searching for him. The alert described Shane Nguyen to be a 55 year old who was 5’ 9”, about 142 lbs, having black hair and brown eyes. Information about his vehicle, a black 2013 Honda Odyssey Van with an Indiana license plate VSU544, was included as well. It was believed that he was in extreme danger and may require medical assistance.</p>

<p>Prior to the missing report Nguyen was approximately last seen at 3pm at the Allen County Memorial Coliseum after receiving a dose of a COVID-19 vaccine. Nguyen’s family and friends were hopeful that he would be found and would return home safely. </p>
